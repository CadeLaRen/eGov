Welcome to eGov SmartCity eGovernance Suite

eGov SmartCity eGovernance suite of products aim to improve the internal efficiency, transparency, accountability and the service delivery of Municipal Governments. The solution is freely available to all under GPL V3 license. The suite of products available are: Revenue Modules (Property Tax, Water Charges, Advertisement Charges, Trade License, Land and Estate), Expenditure (Works Management, Payroll & Pension, Inventory Management, Asset Management), Citizen Convenience ( Grievance Redressal, Birth and Death, Building Plan Approval, Marriage Registration) and Admin Efficiency (Employee Information, File Management, Legal Case Management, Council Management).

The solution is completely open source and is based on open source technologies. The municipalities across the world can deploy these solutions and go online with their operations in the shortest time without building the solutions ground up.
