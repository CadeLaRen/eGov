Introduction

 

You can use a few or all the modules of the ERP Suite depending on your business requirements. Also, there are various submodules in each module. In each submodule, you have different set of features.

 

Example:

In the ERP Suite, you have modules such as eGov Financials and eGov PTIS. In the eGov Financials, you have submodules such as Masters and Transactions. In the Masters submodule, you have features such as Chart of Accounts and Banks.

 

Using the Gov Administration module, you can add modules, submodules, and new features to your ERP Suite at any point in time.
