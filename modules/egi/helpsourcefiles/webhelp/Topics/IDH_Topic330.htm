Moving or Deleting a Boundary

 

To move a boundary from one area to another or to delete a boundary, perform the following steps:

1.   Log on to the Administration System.

2.   Click Jurisdiction from the left navigation bar.

3.   Click Move Boundary. he Boundary List is displayed.

NOTE: Click the Plus button  to list the subboundaries.

 

4.   Right click the name of the boundary from the Boundary List. You get a dialog box with two options.

5.   Click one of the following options:

·    Shift Boundary: click this option if you want to move a boundary from one area to another and then go to Step 6.

·    Delete Boundary: to delete a boundary

6.   Select an area where you want to move the boundary, from the Move To drop-down list and click Move.
You get an acknowledgement that the boundary has been successfully moved.
