Modifying a Bank

 

You may need to add the details of a bank that  you have created.

 

To modify details of a bank, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Masters to display the sub-menu tree.

2. Click Chart of Accounts > Add/Modify Branch - Modify Bank to display a page where you can enter details regarding the bank you want to modify.

3. Select the name of the bank from the Bank Name drop-down list.

4. Click Modify to display the details.

You can change the values to suit your requirements. For field descriptions, see the tables.

5. Click one of the following options:

·      Save and New to save the information and modify another branch details

·      Save and Close to save the information and close the window.
