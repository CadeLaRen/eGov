Introduction

 

While accounting a contractor’s bill, the ULB may have to make several statutory deductions such as income tax at source, works contract tax, contribution to contractors’ benevolent fund, and royalty. The type and percentage of deductions depends upon the type, value, and effective date of the contract, and the prevailing statutory requirements.

 

For example, the provident fund being collected from employees' salary needs to be remitted to the bank.

 

The “Remittance Recoveries” setup facilitates masters to be created for all such deductions required to be made from contractors’ bills from time to time.

 

The recovery types created in this setup appear as a drop-down list in the "Type" field in the "Deductions" section of the “Contractors Bill Create” screen. On selection of a type, deduction is auto-calculated, and is displayed in the amount column. As many deductions as required can be made from the bill. The net amount after deducting all recoveries from the bill passed amount is automatically credited to the the contractor's account.
