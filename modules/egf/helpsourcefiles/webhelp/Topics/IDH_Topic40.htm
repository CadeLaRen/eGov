Introduction

 

The initial phase of the budget creation is to define the budget. In the budget definition, you can specify the name of the budget and the financial year for which the budget is created.

 

You can create individual budgets for each department and collate these individual budgets to constitute a primary budget of your organization.

 

For example, you want to create a budget for the financial year 2010-11 for your ULB. Assume that there are 3 departments in your ULB; say Department 1, Department 2, and Department 3. Each department may have its own revenue and expenditure. Therefore, you need to define a budget for each department; say Department1Budget2009-10, Department2Budget2009-10, and Department3Budget2009-10. ULB budget is the combination of each department budget.
