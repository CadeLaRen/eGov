Creating a Salary Bill Voucher

 

After approving a salary bill, you can generate a voucher.

 

To generate a voucher for a salary bill, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Transactions> Expenditure > Bills Accounting> Salary Bill to display a page, displaying the following search criteria to find a bill:

·      Bill Date From

·      Bill Date To

·      Bill Number

·      Month

2. Enter the details as above and Search to list the salary bills.

3. Select the check box against the bill whose voucher you want to generate.

4. Click Generate Voucher to display the Salary Journal Create page where you must select a fund from the Fund drop-down list.

5. Click one of the following options:

·      Save and New to save the changes and create voucher for another salary bill

·      Save and Close to save the changes and close the window

·      Close to close the Window

 

On successful completion, a voucher number is displayed on your screen. To confirm the voucher, see Confirming a Voucher.

 

All earning pay heads are debited, deductions are credited, and net amount are credited.

 

Amount Credited to Salary Payable Account = Total Earnings - Total Deductions.
