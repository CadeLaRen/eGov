Reversing Contractor/Supplier/Salary/Expense Bill Vouchers

 

Once a bill is approved and the bill voucher created, the bill cannot be cancelled. To nullify the effect of the bill, it needs to be reversed.

 

To reverse a bill voucher, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Transactions> Expenditure > Bills Accounting> Reverse Bills to display the Reverse Bills Search page, displaying the following search criteria to find a voucher:

·      Bill Type

·      Voucher Number

·      Fund

·      Voucher Date From

·      Voucher Date To

·      Department

 

2. Enter the details as described in the table and Search to list the vouchers.

3. Click on the voucher number that you want to reverse.

 

You get a page displaying the voucher details.

 

4. Click Reverse to display the Voucher Date field where you need to enter the new voucher date.

5. Click Submit to finish and display the voucher details on your screen.

 

NOTE: Only vouchers of "approved" status can be reversed.

 

Table: Reverse Bill Voucher

 

Field

Description

Voucher Number From

Type the lower range of the voucher number

Voucher Number To

Type the upper range of the voucher number

Fund

Fund associated with the transaction

Voucher Date From

Start date of the voucher. For example, to view a bill voucher that was created after Jan 1, 2010, then type 01/01/2010.

Voucher Date To

End date of the voucher. 

Department

Enter the name of the department which created the bill
