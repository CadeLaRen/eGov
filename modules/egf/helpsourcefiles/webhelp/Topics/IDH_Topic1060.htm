Approving a Bill Voucher

 

All created bill vouchers will be available to the relevant officers for approval.

 

To approve a bill voucher, perform the following steps:

 

1. Log on to the application as an approving officer.

 

You get the Inbox where all tasks that need your approval are listed.

 

The Inbox includes the following information:

·        Date and time at which you received the task in your Inbox

·        Position and user name of the sender

·        Name of the task

·        Status

·        Details of the task

 

2. Click the name of the voucher that you want to approve.

 

You get a page where all details of the voucher appear. You can verify the adequacy of this data and decide whether or not to sanction it.

 

3. In the Approval Information section, enter the information as described in Table 1. This section will be available only if you choose to employee a manual workflow.

 

4. Click one of the following options:

·        REJECT: To reject the voucher in case you find the data is inadequate. The rejected vouchers will automatically be sent to the Inbox of the person who created it, for further modification.

·        APPROVE: To approve/forward the voucher.

 

NOTE: When you click APPROVE, the voucher will be sent to the Inbox of the next officer for further approval, unless you are the final approving authority.

 

Once a voucher is approved, you are read to go ahead and create a bank payment voucher.

 

Table 1: Approval Information

Field Name

Field Description

Approver Department

Select the department to which the officer who needs to approve the bill belongs.

Approver Designation

A list of designations pertaining to the selected department is displayed. The list will include only those designations that belong to the next level of hierarchy. You can select a designation from the drop-down list.

Approver

Based on the designation you have selected, a list of users who are mapped to the designation is displayed. You can select a user name from the drop-down list.

Comments

Type any comment that you want to include.
