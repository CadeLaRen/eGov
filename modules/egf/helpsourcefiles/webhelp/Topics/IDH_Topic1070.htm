Direct Bank Payment

 

In addition to the payment that is made to contractors and suppliers, ULBs can make payments for miscellaneous purposes such as travel expenses of the commissioner and for other unplanned events. Such expenses are not backed with a bill. You can use the Direct Bank Payment feature for accounting such miscellaneous payments.

 

To enter details of a direct bank payment, perform the following steps:

 

1. Log on to the application using the relevant credentials.

2. Click Transactions > Expenditure > Payments > Direct Bank Payment. You get the Create Direct Bank Payment screen, which has the following four sections:

·         Header Details

·         Account Details

·         Sub-Ledger Details

·         Approval Information

3. In the Header Details section, enter the details as described in Table 1.

4. In the Account Details section, enter the details as described in Table 2.

5. In the Sub-Ledger Details section, enter the details as described in Table 3.

6. In the Approval Information section, enter the details as described in Table 4. NOTE: This section will be available only if you have chosen to employee a manual approval workflow.

7. Click one of the following options:

·         Save and Forward 

·         Close

 

NOTE: When you click Save and Forward, the task will be sent to the Inbox of the next officer for further approval with an appropriate UI message. The Approve option will be available to you only if you are the final approving officer.
