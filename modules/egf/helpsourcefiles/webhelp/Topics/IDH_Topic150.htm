Approving a Budget

 

1. Log on to the application. You get the Inbox where all tasks that need your approval are listed.

 

The Inbox includes the following information:

 

    * Date and time at which you received the task in your Inbox.

    * Position and user name of the sender.

    * Name of the budget to be approved.

 

2. Click the name of the budget you want to approve. You get the Budget Search and Modify page, where you have various search criteria to find the budget you want to approve.

3. Click the name of the budget to bring up the details. You can verify the correctness of the data and decide whether or not to approve it.

4. Click one of the following options:

 

·         Submit for Approval to approve the budget. The budget will be forwarded to the concerned officer for further approval, if any.

·         Save to save the budget. The budget will be available for further corrections in Drafts.
