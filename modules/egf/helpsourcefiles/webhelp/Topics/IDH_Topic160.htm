Introduction

 

Reappropriation is the process of modifying the estimated figures of an approved budget in tune with change in business requirements. You can create budget details of a new budget group too while performing the reappropriation.

 

You can choose to have budget reappropriation done on any BE/RE.

 

You can reappropriate a budget any number of times during a financial year.

 

The budget that has undergone reappropriation requires to go through approval cycles.
