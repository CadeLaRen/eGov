Introduction

 

In the previous chapter, you have figured out the anticipated transactions and included them as part of a department budget. Now, you need to specify the proposed amount for each of the transactions.

 

For example, you have defined a budget, say Department1Budget_2009-10, and you have created the two budget groups, Department1_TravelExpense_2009-10 and Department1_RoadTax_Income_2009-10.

 

Referring to the example above, you need to specify the following details in the budget:

·      amount that is proposed to be spent for employees for business travel in 2009-10

·      amount that is proposed to be earned by the department from road tax collection in 2009-10

 

Setting up the budget details includes the following:

·      Creating budget details

·      Modifying budget details

·      Viewing budget details
