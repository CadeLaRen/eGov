Recording Cash Withdrawal Details

 

This is used to withdraw cash from the ULB bank account on an as required basis, typically done for petty cash expenditure on an imprest account.

 

To record the details of cash that was withdrawn from a bank, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Transactions > Contra Entries > Cash Withdrawal to display the Bank to Cash Transfer page.

2. Enter the details as described in the table.

3. Click Save to save the information.

 

Table: Cash Withdrawal Deposit Details

Field Name

Description

Bank

Name of the bank from where you withdraw money.

Voucher Date

The current date is displayed. You can change the date.

Account Number

Your account number is automatically displayed.

Amount

The amount that you want to withdraw

Available balance

Balance amount held at the bank. This field is automatically displayed.

Cheque Number

Cheque number if the money is withdrawn through a cheque

Cheque Date

Date of creation of the cheque

Fund

The name of the fund is automatically displayed, depending on the bank you have selected.

Department

Select the name of the department associated with the transaction.

Scheme

Select the name of the scheme associated with the cash deposit.

Subscheme

Select a subscheme if any.

Functionary

Select a functionary from the drop-down list.

Fund Source

Select a fund source from the drop-down list.

Field

Select a ward number associated with the fund. Example: Property Tax for Ward 10.

Narration

A brief description.
