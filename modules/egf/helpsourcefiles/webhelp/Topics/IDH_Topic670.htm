Reversing a Contra Voucher

 

Sometimes, you may need to reverse a contra entry that has been already created and approved. The system automatically nullifies the effect of the entries by creating reversal entries.

 

NOTE: Only approved contra vouchers can be reversed.

 

To reverse a contra entry, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Transactions > Contra Entries > Reverse Contra Entries to display the Voucher Search page.

2. Enter the details as described in the table.

3. Click Search to display the vouchers that meet the given search criteria.

4. Click the voucher that you want to reverse. The details of the voucher are displayed.

5. Enter the new reversal voucher number in the Reversal Voucher Number field.

6. Enter the date of creation of reversal voucher date in the Reversal Voucher Date field.

7. Click one of the following options:

·      Reverse and New to save the details and reverse another voucher.

·      Reverse and View to reverse the voucher and view the details.

·      Reverse and Close to reverse the voucher and close the window.

·      Close to close the window without saving any changes.
