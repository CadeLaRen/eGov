Approving a Bank Payment Voucher (BPV)

 

All BPVs need to be approved by the concerned authorities before they go for payments.

 

NOTE: Before approving a BPV, to find whether or not the bank through which the payment has to be made has sufficient running balance, see the Outstanding Bank Payments report.

 

To approve a bank payment voucher, perform the following steps:

1. Log on to the application as an officer who has the privilege to approve a payment voucher.

 

All items that need to be approved will be displayed in the Inbox.

 

The Inbox includes the following information:

·        Date and time at which you received the task in your Inbox

·        Position and user name of the sender

·        Name of the task

·        Status

·        Details of the task

 

2. Click the line item that you want to sanction.

 

You get the Bill Payment View page which includes all details of the payment such as the voucher number, voucher date, name of the branch and bank, and the amount to be paid. A full trail of the task is also displayed at the end of the page.

 

You can verify the adequacy of this data and decide whether or not to sanction it. You can also leave a comment in the Comments field.

 

3. Click one of the following options:

·        Forward/Approve: To approve/forward the payment voucher.

·        Reject: To reject the payment voucher in case you find the data is inadequate. The rejected items will automatically be sent to the Inbox of the person who created it, for further modification. The person can cancel the voucher as well.

 

NOTE: When you click Forward, the item will be sent to the Inbox of the next officer for further approval with an appropriate UI message. The Approve button will be available to you only if you are the final approving officer.

 

A payment voucher is said to be "approved" only when all officers have approved it. Once the voucher is approved, the assistant can assign a check.
