Introduction

 

ULBs perform thousands of transactions every day through banks. At a given point, the bank statements may not be current in line with the accounting records of the ULB. Bank reconciliation enables ULBs to compare their account records to the bank's records of their account balance in order to uncover any possible discrepancies. A bank reconciliation done regularly can reduce the number of errors in an accounts system and make it easier to find missing purchases and sales invoices.
