Dishonored Cheque Details

 

It may so happen that the cheques submitted by either a citizen or ULB are dishonored because of such reasons as insufficient funds and wrong cheque details. You need to track the details of such cheques.

 

To enter information regarding a dishonored cheque, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Transactions > Expenditure > BRS > Dishonored Cheques to display a page, displaying the following search criteria:

·      Name of the bank and the branch where the cheque was presented.

·      Account number that you hold in the selected bank

·      Cheque Number

·      Cheque Date

2. Click View to display the details.

 

Table: Dishonored Cheques 

Field Name

Description

Bank and Branch

Name of the bank and branch whose cheque was dishonored

Account Number

Account number that you hold with the selected bank

Cheque Number

Number of the dishonored cheque

Cheque Date

Date of creation of the cheque
