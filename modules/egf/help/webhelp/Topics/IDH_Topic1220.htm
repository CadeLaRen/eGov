Receipt/Payment Report

 

This report is similar to trial balance to the extent that, it gives a summarized and classified report of transactions as on a particular date. The difference is that “trial balance” deals with transactions as on a particular date, whereas the ‘Receipts and Payments Statement” deals with only cash and bank transactions. Another difference is that the trial balance does not show opening and closing balances, whereas the Receipts and Payments Statement starts with opening cash & Bank balances, and ends with closing cash & bank balances. It follows the logic:

 

Opening cash & bank balances + Receipts  =  Payments +  Closing  cash & bank balances

If the Receipts and Payments Statement does not pass this test, it indicates inaccuracy either in the report itself, or in the entries in the books of account.

 

To generate a Receipt/Payment Report, perform the following steps:

1. Click Reports > Financial Statements > Receipt/Payment Report.

2. Enter the details as described in Table 1.

3. Click Submit to generate the report.

You can export the report in both Excel and PDF formats.

 

Table 1: Receipt/Payment Report

Field

Description

Period*

Select one of the following options:

·             Yearly

·             Half-Yearly

·             Monthly

Year*

Select an year for which you want to generate the report.

Amount In*

Select a unit to express the amount.
