Sub-Ledger Schedule Report

 

A sub-ledger schedule is a listing of balances in a particular sub-ledger. The total sub-ledger balance should tally with the balance in the control account in the General Ledger, as explained in the Sub-ledger Report.  A sub-ledger schedule has the same relationship to a Sub-ledger, as the trial balance has to the general ledger. It indicates the arithmetical accuracy of entries in the Sub-ledger, and also provides a listing of the dr/cr balances in the sub-ledger.

 

A separate sub-ledger is generated for each sub-ledger, and lists out the balances in the entities in that sub-ledger. For example, a Suppliers Sub-ledger Schedule lists out all the suppliers balances as on a particular date and the total tallies with the balance in the Creditors-Suppliers Payable (381100). Similarly, the Contractors-Sub-ledger Schedule lists out all contractors’ balances as on a particular date, and the total tallies with the balance in the Creditors-Contractors Payable (381200).

 

To generate a Sub-Ledger Schedule Report, perform the following steps:

1. Click Reports > Financial Statements > Sub-Ledger Schedule.

2. Enter the details as described in Table 1.

3. Click Submit to generate the report.

You can export the report in both Excel and PDF formats.

 

Table 1: Sub-Ledger Schedule Report

Field

Description

Account Code*

Select an account code.

Fund*

Name of the fund.

Starting Date*

Date starting when you want to generate the report.

End Date*

Date till when you want to generate the report.

Sub Ledger Type*

Select a sub-ledger type, depending on the specified account code.
