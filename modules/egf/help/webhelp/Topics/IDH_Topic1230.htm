Balance Sheet Report

 

The balance sheet gives the financial position of a ULB at the end of a given period.

 

To generate a Balance Sheet Report, perform the following steps:

1. Click Reports > Financial Statements > Balance Sheet.

2. Enter the details as described in Table 1.

3. Click Submit to generate the report.

You can export the report in both Excel and PDF formats.

 

Table 1: Balance Sheet Report

Description

·             Yearly

·             Half-Yearly
