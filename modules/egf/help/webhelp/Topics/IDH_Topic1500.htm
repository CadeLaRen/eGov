Advance Payment

 

Advance disbursement is a common practice followed in every department of the ULB. Employees can avail of salary advances, contractors/suppliers can avail of advances against contractor/supplier bills. The respective department is required to create a bill against an advance requisition form submitted by the relevant party. The party can be contractors, suppliers, or employees. The bill must be approved by the officials concerned. The approved bill is forwarded to the Financials module as an Inbox item for BPV generation and check assignment.

 

Advance payment work flow includes the following steps:

·         Creation of a bill against an advance requisition form (for example, if the advance is disbursed to a contractor, a bill is created in the Works module)

·         Approval of the bill by the officers concerned of the respective department and submission of the bill to Financials.

·         Creation of a BPV followed by budget checking and fund availability

·         Approval of the BPV (number of approval cycle is implementation-specific)

·         Assignment of a cheque

 

NOTE: The last three steps take place in the Financials module.

Generating a BPV

 

To generate a BPV for an advance payment, perform the following steps:

 

1. Log on to the application using the appropriate login credentials.

 

Advance Requisition Forms (ARFs) originating from different modules are displayed in your Inbox.

 

2. Double click the ARF whose BPV you want to generate.

You get the Advance Requisition Form page which displays all details about the advance that needs to be disbursed.

 

3. Click Create BPV to display a page where you have the following sections:

·         Payment Details

·         Approver Information

 

4.  In the Payment Details section, enter the details as described in the the table 1.

 

5. In the Approval Information section, enter the following details of the person to whom the BPV has to be forwarded for approval:

·         Department to which the approver belongs

·         Designation of the approver

·         User name of the approver

 

6. Click Generate BPV. You get a message indicating that the BPV has been created and that it has been forwarded to the specified officer for approval.

 

NOTE: Using the eGov Admin module, you can set up either a manual or an automatic workflow that suits best for your business requirements. If you have chosen to use a manual workflow, while creating the BPV, you need to select the department and designation of the person who needs to approve it. When you use an automatic workflow, you do not need to manually select these fields. The system automatically forwards the BPV to the relevant users for approval.

 

Table: Generate BPV for Advance Payments

·     

Field

Description

Bank Name

Name of the bank through which the payment needs to be made

Account Number

Account number that you hold with the bank

Mode of Payment

The mode of payment can be cash, cheque, or other modes

Payment Amount

The amount that needs to be paid as advance.

Voucher Number

You need to enter the voucher number only if you have not chosen to employee an automatic voucher number creation.

Voucher Date

The date when the voucher was created

Narration

Any brief description about the advance disbursal.
