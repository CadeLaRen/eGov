Preface

 

eGov Financials is an application that takes care of all needs of the Financial Accounting department in a ULB.

 

Intended Audience

Information furnished in this guide is for the exclusive use of ULB officials who are engaged in any aspect of financial accounting.

 

Implementation Details

This guide is generic in nature and may not cover all features specific to your implementation. For implementation-specific details, see the configuration guide provided along with the product.
