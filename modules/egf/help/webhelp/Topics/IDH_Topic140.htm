Introduction

 

Each department head prepares a budget for his/her department and sends it to the financial department. The financial department consolidates the individual budgets to a single budget and forwards it to the concerned officers for their approval. The officers get the budgets for approval in their Inbox. They must verify the adequacy of data specified in each line item before approving the budget. A budget will be considered to be approved only when all the line items are verified and approved by the concerned officer. In case the officers find any discrepancy in any of the line items, they can summarily reject the budget after leaving a remark. Such budgets are sent back to the person who created them for necessary modifications.

 

NOTES:

 

·      There can be multiple levels of approvals depending on your business requirements.

·      Only primary budgets and the associated child budgets will be sent for approval.

Submitting the Budgets for Approval

 

To submit the budget details for approval, perform the following steps:

1. To find the details of the budget that you want to submit for approval, enter one or more of the following information in the associated fields:

·      Financial year for which the budget was created

·      Name of the budget

·      Name of the department that executes the project

·      Function

·      Fund

2. Click Search to display the budgets that meet the given search criteria.

3. Click the name of the budget. You get the Budget Details page, which lists all information according to the budget you have selected.

4. To change the proposed amount for the next financial year, type a value in the Budget Estimate Proposed field.

5. Click one of the following options:

·      Submit For Approval to forward the budget to the sanctioning officers.

·      Save to save the budget and forward it for approval at a later time.
