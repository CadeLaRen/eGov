Approving a Salary Bill

 

You need to approve the salary bill that you have generated, before generating a voucher.

 

To approve a salary bill, perform the following steps:

1. Click Transactions> Expenditure > Bill Registers > Salary Bills > Approve Salary Bill to display the Approve Salary Bill-Search page, which displays the following criteria to search for a bill:

 

·      starting bill date

·      ending date

·      Month

·      Department

 

2. Enter the above criteria and click Search to list out the bills that meet the criteria (see table below).

3. Select the check box against the bill you want to approve, and click Approve.

 

You get a message, indicating that the bill has been approved.

 

You can type any comments in the Comments field if necessary.

 

Table: Approve Salary Bills

Field

Description

Starting Bill Date

The start date of creation of a bill

Ending Date

The start date of creation of a bill

Month

Month for which you want to view the salary bill

Department

Department for which the salary bill is being processed.
