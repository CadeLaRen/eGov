Approving Bills and Creating Vouchers

 

You need to get the bills approved by the concerned authority before processing them.

 

To approve a bill, perform the following steps:

 

1. Log on to the application as an approving officer.

2. Click Transactions> Expenditure > Bill Registers> Approve Bill Registers.

 

You get the Inbox where all bills that need your approval are listed.

3. Click the bill that you want to approve.

 

You get the details of the bill such as:

·      bill number

·      bill amount

·      bill date

·      expenditure type

·      bill status

·      approval date

·      Observation

 

You can verify the adequacy of this data and decide whether or not to sanction it. You can leave a comment in the Observation field if you wish to do so.

 

4. Select one of the following check boxes:

·      Accept if you want to approve the bill

·      Reject if you want to reject the bill

5. If you want to accept the bill, type the date of approval in the Approval Date field.

6. Click Submit.

 

A voucher for the selected bill will automatically be generated in the system. To confirm the voucher, see Confirming a Voucher.

 

NOTE: Only bills created in Financials require this approval process. The bills that have been created in other modules will go through a different workflow.
