Viewing Account Codes

 

All accounting entries must be identified by a standard set of account codes that is typically organized in a tree structure.  

 

To view the account codes, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Masters to display the sub-menu tree.

2. Click Chart of Accounts to display a page listing the following parent account heads:

·      Income

·      Expenses

·      Liabilities

·      Assets

3. To view a major code:

·      Click the corresponding parent account head to list out the major account heads.

·      Click the name of the major head whose account code you want to view.

 

For example, assume that you want to view the account code of the major head, "Property Tax."

a) Click "Income" to list out the major income account heads such as Property Tax, Water Tax.

b) Click "Property Tax" to display the details, including account code and account name.

 

Similarly, you can view the sub-minor and detailed account codes.
