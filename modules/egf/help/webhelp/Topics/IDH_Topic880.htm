Introduction

 

A bill voucher can have the following statuses:

 

·      Created: It means that the voucher is created in the system. When the voucher is in this state, you can modify, cancel, and confirm the vouchers.

·      Confirmed: When the voucher is in this state, payment can be generated. You cannot cancel a Confirmed voucher, but can reverse it.

·      Cancelled:  When the voucher is in this state, it is deemed to be cancelled, but it is not deleted to comply with audit requirements. 

·      Reversed:  Reverses the effect of the voucher as of the reversal date by passing a new voucher with the opposite effect as the original voucher.
