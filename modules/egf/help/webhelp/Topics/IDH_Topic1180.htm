Surrendering/Reassigning Cheques

 

Occasionally, it may so happen that a party returns a cheque to the ULB without encashing it, because of reasons such as:

·         Incorrect cheque details (incorrect name, account number) due to which bank may not have made the payment

·         Cancellation of the payment by the ULB

 

When a cheque is surrendered due to incorrect details, ULB must re-assign a cheque to the party.

 

To enter details on a surrendered cheque, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Transactions > Expenditure > Payments > Surrender/Reassign Cheque.  You get the Surrender Cheque Search page which displays the following search criteria to find a cheque:

·         Payment Voucher Date From

·         Payment Voucher Date To

·         Payment Voucher No

·         Bank

·         Bank Account

·         Department

 

2. Enter the details as above and click Search to list out the cheques that meet the given search criteria.

3. Select the Surrender check box against the cheque that you want to surrender.

4. Click one of the following options:

·         Surrender to surrender the cheque without reassigning a new cheque to the party

·         Surrender and and Reassign to surrender the cheque and reassign a new cheque to the party

 

NOTE: As soon as you click Surrender and Reassign, the system automatically assigns a new cheque to the party. You get a message indicating so. When you click Surrender, the cheque associated with the payment voucher will be removed.
