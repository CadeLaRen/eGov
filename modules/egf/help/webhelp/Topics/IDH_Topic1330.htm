Reversing a Bank Payment Voucher (BPV)

 

You may reverse an approved BPV whenever the situation warrants it.

 

To reverse a BPV, perform the following steps:

 

1. Click Transactions > Expenditure > Payments > Reverse Payments.  You get the Payment Search page which displays the following search criteria to find a BPV:

·         Mode of Payment

·         Voucher Number

·         Fund

·         Department

·         Fund Source

·         Payment Voucher Date From

·         Payment Voucher Date To

·         Bank

·         Account Number

 

2. Enter the details as above and click Search to list out the BPVs that meet the given search criteria. The list includes details such as:

·         Voucher Number

·         Voucher Name

·         Voucher Date

·         Amount

 

3. Click the voucher number of the BPV that you want to reverse. You get the Create Direct Payment page which includes all details about the payment.

4. Enter the date of reversal in the Reversal Voucher Date field.

5. Click one of the following options:

·         Reverse and View to reverse the PBV and view the details

·         Reverse and Close to reverse the PBV and close the Window

·         Close to close the Window

 

You get a message confirming that the BPV has been reversed.
