Registering Complaints Via Phone

 

To register a complaint via phone, you need to make a call to the number specified on the city Web site. The complaint cell operator records your complaints in the PGR System.

 

You need to provide the following details to the complaint cell operator:

·      Name and initials

·      Street Address

·      Pin Code

·      Phone Number

·      Mobile Number

·      E-mail Address

·      Name of the Area

·      Name of the Locality

·      Name of the Street

 

The Complaint Cell Operator enters and saves the information that you have provided. Thereafter, the operator gives you a unique complaint number. You can use this number to track your complaint.
