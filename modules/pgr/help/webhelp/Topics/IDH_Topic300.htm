Generating Department Wise Status Reports

 

This feature is used by a city official who is either a Grievance Officer or a Municipal Officer to generate status reports on complaints registered under various departments.

 

To generate department wise status reports, perform the following steps:

1.   Log on to the PGR System.

2.   Under Status Reports, click the Department-Complaint Type Wise Status Reports hyperlink. A page with the title Pick Date for Department Wise Status Report is displayed with the following fields:

·    Start/On Date

·    End Date

·    Department

3.   To generate reports for different time spans, provide information in the fields as follows:

·    To generate a report as of the current date, leave the Start/On Date and End Date fields empty.

·    To generate a report from a specific date till the current date, complaints enter the Start/On Date field.

·    To generate a report till specific date, enter the End Date field.

·    To generate a report for between two dates, enter both the dates.

 

NOTE: Selection on department is mandatory.

 

4.   Select the appropriate department from the Department drop-down list.

5.   Click Submit to generate the department wise status report.

6.   Click Print to take a printout of the report.
