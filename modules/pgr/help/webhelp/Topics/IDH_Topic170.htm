Introduction

 

You can track your complaint over Internet or phone and find the status by using the complaint number.

 

Your complaint can be in any of the following statuses:

·      Registered: Complaint is under process.

·      Forwarded: Complaint is forwarded to another official.

·      Processing: Complaint is under process.

·      Completed: Action has been taken on your complaint.

·      Rejected: Complaint has been rejected.

·      Withdrawn: Complaint has been withdrawn by the citizen.

·      Reopen: Complaint has been reopened by the citizen.

 

There may be situation where your complaint is in the "Completed" status, while no action has actually been taken on it by the authority. In such a case, you can reopen the complaint.

 

You can post any additional comments regarding your complaint if you desire to do so. You can also withdraw your complaint whenever the situation demands it.

 

NOTE: A citizen can either withdraw or reopen a complaint at any point of time.
