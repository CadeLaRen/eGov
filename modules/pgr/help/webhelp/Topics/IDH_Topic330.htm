Introduction

 

This feature is used by a city official who is either a Grievance Officer or a Municipal Officer to generate status reports on all the complaints registered under various departments and wards. These reports list the status wise number of complaints for all departments or wards between given dates or as on current date.

 

You can generate two types of aggregated status reports as follows:

·    Department Wise Status Reports

·    Ward Wise Status Reports
