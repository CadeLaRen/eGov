Overview of City Administration Tasks

 

The eGov PGR System features for City Administration can be accessed by city officials who have the required permissions. Such city officials will have a user name and password assigned to them.

 

The city officials include the following:

·      Grievance Officer

·      Municipal Officer

·      Complaint Cell Operator

·      Administrator

 

These officials will take appropriate measures on citizen complaints as follows:

·      View and follow up on complaints.

·      Take appropriate action and close the complaint.

·      Forward complaints to the relevant officer in the relevant department.

·      Generate reports for a specific department and a specific ward as needed.

·      Create master data. For more information, see Setting Up the Master Data.
