Introduction

 

The several ways in which you can register a complaint on any aspect of the government's working in a particular city are as follows:

·      Using the Internet.

·      Using the telephone.

·      Filling the form available at the Grievance Center for citizens. Alternatively, you can go to the city Web site, access the complaint registration form, take a printout, fill it up, and submit it at the Grievance Center.

·      Sending an SMS mentioning the complaint details.

 

To register a complaint, perform the following steps:

1.   Go to www.chennaicorporation.gov.in. You get the city Web site home page.

2.   Click the Online Public Grievances hyperlink. You get the PGR home page which contains the following hyperlinks to register your complaints:

 

·    Registering Complaints via Internet: To register your complaints over the Internet.

·    Registering Complaints via Phone: To register your complaints through a phone.

·    Registering Complaints via Paper Form: To register your complaints by filling the form and submitting it to an official.

·    Registering Complaints via SMS: To register your complaints by sending a Short  Message Service (SMS).

 

After a complaint is registered using any of the methods mentioned above, the citizen receives a complaint number. For example, COC-00234-ABC.

 

NOTE: The complaint number is required for you to track your complaints and therefore it needs to be safeguarded. If you lose the complaint number, you must contact the complaint cell operator to retrieve the number.
