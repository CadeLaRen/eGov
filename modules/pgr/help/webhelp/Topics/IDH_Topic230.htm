Introduction

 

After a citizen registers a complaint, it is routed to the relevant Municipal Officer who is responsible for addressing the complaint. Once the complaint is addressed, the complaint is closed.

 

When either the Complaint Type or the Complaint Location is not specified by the citizen, the complaint is forwarded to the inbox of the Grievance Officer who then, forwards it to the associated Municipal Officer.

 

A Grievance Officer can set priority to a complaint at his/her discretion. In addition, the officer can also update the complaint details such as department name, zone name, and so on.

 

A city official can also update the complaints that have been received in his/her inbox.
