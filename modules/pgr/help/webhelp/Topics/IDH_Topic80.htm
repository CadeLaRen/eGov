Introduction

 

Before using the Public Grievance Redressal (PGR) application, the administrator must create the master data such as complaint group, complaint type and complaint receiving center so that this data is available for the city officials while performing any task.

Citizens can register different types of complaints. In order to channelize these complaints to the relevant departments, the complaints are divided into groups such as health, revenue, and solid waste management. For example, the complaint types such as dog menace, stagnation of water, and rise of epidemics can be put in the complaint group -"Health".

 

The complaint can be received at different centers. For paper based complaints, the receiving center can be one of the following:

·  Mayor

·  CM Office

·  Zone Office

·  Commissioner Office

·  Complaint Cell

 

For all other types of registration of complaints, the default receiving center is the complaint cell. The complaint receiving center master data has to be created by the administrator.

 

Setting up the master data includes:

·  Creating/Editing a Complaint Group

·  Creating/Editing a Complaint Type

·  Creating/Editing a Complaint Receiving Center
