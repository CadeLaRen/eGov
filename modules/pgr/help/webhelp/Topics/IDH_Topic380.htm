Viewing Complaint Type Wise Pie Chart

 

To view the complaint type wise pie chart, perform the following steps:

1.   Log on to the PGR System.

2.   Under View PieCharts, click the Complaint Group - Complaint Type Wise Listing drop-down arrow and select any of the following complaint groups:

·    Health

·    General

·    Engineering

·    Revenue

·    Solid Waste Management

3.   Click Go. A page with a pie chart of complaint type wise breakdown under the selected complaint group is displayed with the following information:

·    The different complaint types are represented in different colours on the pie chart.

·    The count and percentage of total number of complaints under each complaint type is specified.

·    The count of completed complaints is specified.
