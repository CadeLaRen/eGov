Updating Complaint Details by a Grievance Officer

 

If a complaint is not routed to the concerned official, you can forward the same to the concerned officer. You can update the complaint details such as the complaint group, complaint type and can also add comments regarding the complaint.

 

NOTE: The Grievance Officer can update any complaint of the following status:

·    Registered

·    Forwarded

·    Processing

 

To update the complaint details, perform the following steps:

1.    Log on to the PGR System.

2.    Click Reports. A page is displayed with the following fields:

·    Date Selection

·    From Date

·    To Date

·    Status

·    Zone

·    Ward

·    Department

·    Users of the Department

·    Receiving Center

·    Other Complaint Types

3.   To find the complaint that needs to be updated, use one or more of the above search criteria.

4.   Click Submit to get a list of complaints according to your search criteria. The complaints are listed in batches of twenty. To view the next twenty complaints, click 2 which indicates page 2 and so on. Click Last to view the last page of complaints.

5.   Click the number of the complaint to be updated from the list. The complaint details page is displayed with the following sections.

·    Complaining Person's Details

·    Complaint Details

·    Complaint Forwardal Details

6.    In the Complaint Details section, click Update to get a page where you can update the field values.

7.   On this page, in the Other Options section, you can do the following:

·    To change the complaint status, select an option from the Change Status drop-down list. The complaint status list includes: Registered, Forwarded, Processing, Completed and Rejected.

·    To change the jurisdiction zone, select the appropriate zone from the Zone drop-down list.

·    To change the jurisdiction ward, select the appropriate ward from the Ward drop-down list.

·    To change the department, select the appropriate department from the Change Dept drop-down list.

·    To change the complaint type, select the appropriate complaint type from the Change Complaint Type drop-down list.

·    To forward the complaint to a specific authority, select the appropriate official from the Forward Complaint To drop-down list.

·    To add more information regarding a complaint, enter a message in the Post Message field.

8.   Click Submit. The complaint details page is updated. The complaint is forwarded to the official of the relevant department.
