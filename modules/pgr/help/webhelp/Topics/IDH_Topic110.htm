Creating a Complaint Receiving Center

 

To create a Complaint Receiving Center, perform the following steps:

1.           Log on to the PGR System.

2.           Expand the PGR folder to list its subfolder.

3.           Expand the PGR Master folder icon to list its subfolders.

4.           Expand the Complaint Receiving center folder icon to display the Create and Update links.

5.           Click Create to display the Create Complaint Receiving Center page.

6.           Specify the following:

·    Complaint Receiving Center Name: type the name of the receiving center you want to create.

·    Complaint Receiving Center Name Local: type the name of the center in local language.

·    Complaint Receiving Center Type: For paper based complaints, the receiving center type can be one of the following: Mayor, CM office, Zone Office, Commissioner Office and Complaint Cell. For all other types, the default receiving center type is Complaint Cell.

·    Complaint Receiving Center Address: type the address of the complaint receiving center in English.

·    Complaint Receiving Center Address Local: type the address of the complaint receiving center in local language.

·    Select Boundary: select the boundary of location of the complaint receiving center from the drop-down list.

7.           Click Create. You get an acknowledgement message:

             Complaint Receiving Center was created successfully. 

8.           Click Close to close the window.

 

 

Editing a Complaint Receiving Center

 

To edit a Complaint Receiving Center, perform the following steps:

1.           Log on to the PGR System.

2.           Expand the PGR folder to list its subfolder.

3.           Expand the PGR Master folder icon to list its subfolders.

4.           Expand the Complaint Receiving center folder icon to display the Create and Update links.

5.           Click Update to display the Complaint Receiving Center List page.

6.           From the Complaint Receiving Center Name drop-down list, select the name of the complaint receiving center which you want to edit.

7.           Click Edit to display Edit Complaint Receiving Center page where you can modify the values to suit your requirement. For field descriptions, see Creating a Complaint Receiving Center.

8.           Click Update. You get an acknowledgement message:

  Complaint Receiving Center was updated successfully. 

9.           Click Close to close the window.
