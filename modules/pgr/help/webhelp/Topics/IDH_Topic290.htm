Introduction

 

Reports provide all information about the complaints. A city official can view the complaint details that are registered in all the departments. Reports are generated to obtain answers to questions such as the following:

 

·  How many complaints are registered under each department and how many are completed?

·  What is the progress of resolution of complaints in various departments and wards?

·  What is the total number of complaints department wise and ward wise across the city?
