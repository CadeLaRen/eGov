PGR Process Flow

 

The Public Grievance Redressal (PGR) process begins when a citizen or a city official enters a complaint about any area of functioning of a government entity.

 

After a citizen registers a complaint, it is routed to the relevant municipal officer who is responsible for addressing the complaint. Once the complaint is addressed the complaint is closed. The citizen can check the status of the complaint they made and provide more details when required. Citizens can also withdraw their complaints if they want to.
