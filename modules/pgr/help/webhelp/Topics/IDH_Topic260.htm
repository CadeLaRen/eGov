Setting Priority to Complaints by a Grievance Officer

 

You can set a priority to the complaint as per requirement. The complaints can be prioritized as high, medium, low and no priority. The complaint number is indicated in red, yellow, green or white respectively depending on its priority.

 

To set a priority to the complaint, perform the following steps:

1.   Log on to the PGR System.

2.   Click Reports. A page is displayed with the following fields:

·    Date Selection

·    From Date

·    To Date

·    Status

·    Zone

·    Ward

·    Department

·    Users of the Department

·    Receiving Center

·    Other Complaint Types

3.   To find the complaint that needs to be prioritized, use one or more of the above search criteria.

4.   Click Submit to get a list of complaints according to the search criteria you have used.

The complaints are listed in batches of twenty. To view the next twenty complaints, click 2 which indicates page 2 and so on. Click Last to view the last page of complaints.

 

NOTE: You can view all the complaints on a single page by clicking List Complaints in Long Format. 

 

5.   Click the number of the complaint to be prioritized from the list. The complaint details page is displayed.

6.   Click one of the following options from the Change Priority drop-down list:

·    High Priority

·    Medium Priority

·    Low Priority

·    No Priority (default option)

7.   Click Go to set the priority.You will receive a confirmation message based on the priority set.
