Registering Complaints by an Official on Behalf of Citizen

 

To register a complaint on behalf of a citizen, perform the following steps:

1.   Log on to the PGR System.

2.   Click the Register Complaints hyperlink to get the complaint registration form. This form has the following sections:

·  Complaining Person's Details

·  Complaint Location

·  Complaint Type

3.   Click one of the following options:

·    Phone: if you have received the complaint details over phone from the citizen.

·    Paper Form: if you have received the complaint details from the citizen in a paper form.

NOTE: In this case, you need to select an option from the Receiving Center drop-down list.

·    Internet: if the complaint is received via Internet. For example, if the official wants to register a personal complaint.

·    SMS: if you have received the complaint details from the citizen via SMS. 

4.   Enter the registration number in the Registration Number field. This number is printed on the complaint registration form.

5.   For descriptions of all other fields, see Registering Complaints via Internet.

6.   Click Submit Complaint. You get the message Your Complaint has been successfully registered on successful submission of the complaint.

 

NOTE: When either the Complaint Type or the Complaint Location is not specified, the complaint is forwarded to the Inbox of the Grievance Officer who then forwards it to the associated Municipal Officer.
