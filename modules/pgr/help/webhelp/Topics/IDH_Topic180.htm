Tracking Complaints via Internet

 

To track your complaint via the Internet, perform the following steps:

1.   Go to www.chennaicorporation.gov.in and click the Online Public Grievance hyperlink to get the PGR System home page.

2.   In the Check your Complaint Status section, enter your complaint number in the Enter Complaint Number field.

3.    Click Go. You get a page that has the following sections:

·    Complaining Person's Details

·    Complaint Details

·    Complaint Messages by the Official

·    Other Options: The fields in this section are editable.
