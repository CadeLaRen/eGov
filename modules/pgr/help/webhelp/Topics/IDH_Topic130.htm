Registering Complaints Via Internet

 

To register your complaint via Internet, perform the following steps:

1.   Go to www.chennaicorporation.gov.in and click the Online Public Grievance hyperlink to get the PGR System home page.

2.   Click the Register Complaints via Internet hyperlink to get the complaint registration form. This form has the following sections:

·        Complaining Person's Details

·        Complaint Location

·        Complaint Type

3.   In the Complaining Person's Details section, enter the following details:

·        Name and initials

·        Street Address

·        Pin Code

·        Phone Number. Format: (city code)(number). For example, if your city is Chennai and the telephone number is 55512121, enter: 044-55512121

·        Mobile Number

·        E-mail Address

4.    In the Complaint Location section, perform the following steps:

a)     To specify the name of the area, select an option from the Area drop-down list.

b)     To specify the name of the locality, select an option from the Locality drop-down list.

c)      To specify the name of the street, select an option from the Street drop-down list.

d)     To specify the name of the locality, select an option from the Locality drop-down list.

e)     To specify any landmark, enter it in the Specific Location field.

5.    In the Complaint Type section, select a complaint from any of the following drop-down lists:

·        Frequently Filed Complaint Types

·        Health

·        General

·        Engineering

·        Revenue

·        Solid Waste Management

6.   If your complaint is not mentioned in any of the above drop-down lists, do the following steps:

a)     Click the Yes option in the Is Other area, and specify the complaint in the If Other Please Specify field.

b)     Enter the title of the complaint in the Complaint Title field.

 

NOTE: The Complaint Title field is automatically filled if you select a complaint from any of

the drop-down lists specified in the Complaint Type section.

 

7.   To enter more description about the complaint, type it in the Details of Complaint field.

8.   Click Submit Complaint. If your complaint is successfully registered, the following message is displayed:

Your complaint has been registered successfully.

You will also receive a unique complaint number. Use this number to track your complaint.

9.   Click one of the following options:

· Print: To take a printout of the form.

· Go to Home Page: To return to the city Web site home page.

· Check Status (visible at the top and bottom of the page): To view the status of your complaint.

· Register Complaints (visible at the top and bottom of the page): To register another complaint.
