Overview of Revenue Official's Tasks

Previous Top Next 

The eGov PTIS application can be accessed by authorised revenue officials using the login accounts assigned to them based on their respective role, access privilege and jurisdiction. The officials include:

·      Assistant

·      Assistant HQ (Headquarter)

·      Tax Collector

·      Assessor

·      Assessor HQ 

·      Assistant Revenue Officer (ARO)

·      Assistant Revenue Officer HQ (ARO HQ)

·      Zonal Officer (ZO)

·      Additional Revenue Officer HQ

·      Revenue Officer (RO)

·      Deputy Commissioner Revenues (DCR)


Each of the officials can perform only some specific tasks. See the table below for more information on the tasks each official can perform:

Revenue Official's Tasks

User

Tasks

Assistant/Assistant HQ

 

NOTE: The Assistant HQ has jurisdiction over the entire city.

·      Create a new property

·      Find a property

·      View a property

·      Transfer the ownership of a property

·      Modify property details that result in increase in tax

·      Modify property details that result in decrease in tax

·      Generate notices 6/7/9/10

Tax Collector/Collection Operator

·      Find a property

·      View a property

·      Collect tax

Assessor/Assessor HQ

 

NOTE: The Assessor HQ has jurisdiction over the entire city.

·      View a property

·      Modify property details that result in increase in tax

·      Modify property details that result in decrease in tax

·      Modify correspondence details

·      Approve/Reject a property that comes in the Inbox for the following tasks:

o   newly created property

o   modify property details that result in increase or decrease in tax
