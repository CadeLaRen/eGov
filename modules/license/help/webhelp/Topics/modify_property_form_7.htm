Modifying Property Details that Result in Increase in Tax

Previous Top Next 

Citizen comes to the City Corporation for updating the details of their property when they undergo some changes. Some of the changes may result in increase in property tax.

 

Such changes are:

·      Increase in the area of the property.

·      Change in the usage of the property from  residential to commercial.

·      Change in the occupancy of the property from self-occupied to tenant-occupied.

·      Change in the construction type from semi-permanent to permanent.
 

When you notice such changes in the property, you must use this feature to modify the property information.

 

To modify property details that result an increase in tax, perform the following steps:

1. Find the property that you want to modify.

2. From the Actions drop-down list, select Notice 7 to display the Modify Property Form 7

3. In the New details section, specify the following:

·           Effective Date: The date on which the property details are changed so as to result in an increase in tax.

·           Property Type: Select the type of property from the drop-down list, and perform one of the following steps:

·    If you have selected Super Structure, perform the following steps:

     a. Select one of the following options from the Owner of the Land drop-down list.

·    Temple Land: the land owned by the Temple Authority.

·    Waqf board Land: the land owned by the Waqf Board.

·    Central Govt. Land: the land owned by the Central Government.

·    State Govt Land: the land owned by the State Government.

·    Church Land: the land owned by the Church Authority.

·    Private Land: the land owned by the Private Authority.

·    Chennai Corporation: the land owned by the Corporation of Chennai. 

·    Others: the land which does not come under any of the above authorities.

    b. In the Land Owner Name field, enter the name of the owner of the land.      

    c. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as described in Table 4.

 

·    If you have selected Vacant Land, perform the following steps:

     a. In the Area of Plot field, enter the total area of the vacant land in square feet.

     b. In the Tax Effective Date field, enter the date (DD/MM/YYYY) effective from when the tax should be calculated. According to the tax rules, tax cannot be raised with retrospect effect beyond 6 years.

 

NOTE: Demand Effective Installment field gets populated with the half-yearly installment from which onwards the tax is due.

 

·    If you have selected Independent Buildings, perform the following steps:

        a. In the Area of Plot field, enter the total area of the plot where the building stands.

        b. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as mentioned in Table 4.

·    If you have selected Flats in Multistoried Buildings, perform the following steps:

        a. In the Undivided share of Land in Sq.feet field, enter the total area of the plot where the building stands.

        b. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as mentioned in Table 4.

·    If you have selected Central Government 33%, Central Government 75%, or State Government, perform the following steps:

        a. In the Area of Plot field, enter the total area of the plot in square feet.

        b. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as mentioned in Table 4. 

·    If you have selected Cell Phone Tower, perform the following steps:

        a. The Cell Phone Tower field is prepopulated with a half-yearly tax amount of Rs. 15,000 and you cannot edit this field.

 

NOTE: The term Cell Phone Towers in the above context refers to the type of property where Base Transceiver Stations (BTS), commonly known as mobile phone towers are installed. For these properties a tax of rupees 15000 has to be collected irrespective of the size and coverage of the BTS.
 

        b. In the Tax Effective Date field, enter the date (DD/MM/YYYY) effective from when the tax should be calculated.  According to the tax rules, tax cannot be raised with retrospect effect beyond 6 years.

 

NOTE: After you enter the tax effective date, the system automatically displays the installment starting from when you need to collect tax in the Demand Effective Installment field.

 

·      If you have selected Cinema theater, perform the following steps:

a. In the Area of Plot field, enter the total area of the plot where the cinema theater stands.

b. From the Category drop-down list, select the category of the cinema theater.

 

NOTE: The cinema theaters are classified as either category A or category B  based on their location and infrastructure. The concerned officer has to go for a site visit and based on the observations classify the cinema theater as either A or B.

 

       c. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as mentioned in Table 4.

                     d. In the Cinema Theater section, specify the following details:

§         Serial No: The serial number

§         Type: The type of the seats in the cinema theater. For example, balcony seats, box seat, gold class, platinum class. It depends on the cinema theater.

§         No. of Units: The number of seats available for each type.

 

NOTE: If a cinema theater has multiple type of seats, click the Plus (+) icon and enter the details. Similarly, click the Cross (X) icon to delete the selected entry.

 

§         Rate Per Unit: The rate per each seat in rupees.

§         Total: The total amount in rupees. This field automatically gets filled in based on the rate per unit and the number of units.

                 e. In the Date of Completion/Occupation field, enter the date (DD/MM/YYYY) effective from when the tax should be calculated.  According to the tax rules, tax cannot be raised with retrospect effect beyond 6 years.

 

NOTE: After you enter the Date of Completion/Occupation, the system automatically displays the installment starting from when you need to collect tax in the Demand Effective Installment field.

 

·      If you have selected Hospital/Nursing Home, perform the following steps:

a. In the Area of Plot field, enter the total area of the plot where the hospital is constructed

b. From the Category drop-down list, select the category to which the hospital belongs.

 

NOTE: The hospitals are classified as either Government or Private.

 

       c. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as mentioned in Table 4.

                     d. In the Hospital section, specify the following details:

§         Serial No: The serial number

§         Type: The type of wards in the hospital. For example, general, semi duplex, duplex. It depends on the Hospital.

§         No. of Units: The number of rooms available for each type.

 

NOTE: If a hospital has multiple type of wards, click the Plus (+) icon and enter the details. Similarly, click the Cross (X) icon to delete the selected entry.

 

§         Rate Per Unit: The rate per each unit in rupees.

§         Total: The total amount in rupees. This field automatically gets filled in based on the rate per unit and the number of units.

                 e. In the Date of Completion/Occupation field, enter the date (DD/MM/YYYY) effective from when the tax should be calculated.  According to the tax rules, tax cannot be raised with retrospect effect beyond 6 years.

 

NOTE: After you enter the Date of Completion/Occupation, the system automatically displays the installment starting from when you need to collect tax in the Demand Effective Installment field.

 

·      If you have selected  Hotel/Lodging House, perform the following steps:

a. In the Area of Plot field, enter the total area of the plot where the hotel or the lodging house is constructed.

b. From the Category drop-down list, select the category to which the hotel or the lodging house belongs.

 

NOTE: The hotels or the lodging houses are classified as either Posh, A Class or B Class.

 

c. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as mentioned in Table 4.

d. In the Star Hotels and Lodging House Details section, specify the following details:

§         Serial No: The serial number

§         Type: The type of rooms in the hotel. For example, single bed, double bed, non AC or AC rooms, suites. It depends on the hotel or the lodging houses.

§         No. of Units: The number of rooms available for each type.

 

NOTE: If a hotel has multiple type of rooms, click the Plus (+) icon and enter the details. Similarly, click the Cross (X) icon to delete the selected entry.

 

§         Rate Per Unit: The rate per each unit in rupees.

§         Total: The total amount in rupees. This field automatically gets filled in based on the rate per unit and the number of units.

                 e. In the Date of Completion/Occupation field, enter the date (DD/MM/YYYY) effective from when the tax should be calculated.  According to the tax rules, tax cannot be raised with retrospect effect beyond 6 years.

 

NOTE: After you enter the Date of Completion/Occupation, the system automatically displays the installment starting from when you need to collect tax in the Demand Effective Installment field.

 

·      If you have selected Kalyana Mandapam, perform the following steps:

a. In the Area of Plot field, enter the total area of the plot where the Kalyana Mandapam is constructed.

b. From the Category drop-down list, select the category to which the Kalyana Mandapam belongs.

 

NOTE: The Kalyana Mandapams are classified as:

·      A Class with vessels 

·      A Class without vessels

·      B Class with vessels 

·      B Class without vessels

·      C Class with vessels  

·      C Class without vessels 

 

       c. In the Floor Details section, specify the details as mentioned in Table 4.

d. In the Rate Charged Per Marriage field, enter the rate (in rupees) charged for conducting a marriage in the Kalyana Mandapam.

e. In the Date of Completion/Occupation field, enter the date (DD/MM/YYYY) effective from when the tax should be calculated.  According to the tax rules, tax cannot be raised with retrospect effect beyond 6 years.

 

NOTE: After you enter the Date of Completion/Occupation, the system automatically displays the installment starting from when you need to collect tax in the Demand Effective Installment field.

 

NOTE: In the Comments field you can enter any description regarding the property.

 

4. Click  Modify Property.

 

You get an acknowledgement page with the following information:

·    The half-yearly tax that is currently paid by the citizen

·    The revised half-yearly tax to be paid starting from the effective date

·    Area of the property (both according to the current record and after revision)

·    Type of the property (current and revised)
 

5.  Click one of the following options:

·    Confirm to confirm the details you have entered.

·    Back to modify the details you have entered.

 

Upon clicking Confirm you get the Notice 7 along with the acknowledgment confirming that the property has been modified. You can choose to print this notice.
