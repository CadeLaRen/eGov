Collecting Property Tax

 

The tax collector needs to collect tax from all property owners in two half-yearly installments as follows:

 

·      1st April to 30th September

·      1st October to 31st March

 

There are two modes of payment options for the citizens, such as cheque and cash payment. They can make the payment in any mode that is convenient for them.

 

When a new property is created, it has to undergo a number of approvals based on the type of property and the area of plot. When a property is approved, the tax collector or the collection operator can access the property tax details in the Inbox. The operator can then collect the tax amount due whenever the citizen approaches to pay the property tax.

 

To collect property tax, perform the following steps:

1.   Log on to the application as an officer who has the right to collect property tax.

2.   Find the property whose tax you want to collect.

3.   From the Actions drop-down list, select Collect Tax to display the Property Details page.

4.   Click Pay Now to display the Collect Property Tax page. The following bill details are displayed:

·  Bill Number

·  Additional Information

·  Payee Name and Address

·  Total Amount Due

 

NOTE: To view the account details such as account code, account name, credit and debit amount, click Account Details. 

 

4.   In the Payment Details section, specify the following details:

 

Table 1:  Payment Details

Field

Description

Mode of Payment

Select one of the following options:

·      Cheque: If the payment is done by cheque.

·      Cash: If the payment is done by cash.

·      DD: If the payment is done by demand draft.

 

NOTE: If there is a cheque bounce, then the Cheque and  Cash options are disabled during the next transaction.

 

DD/Cheque Number

Enter the DD/Cheque Number. 

DD/Cheque Date

Enter the DD/Cheque date.

Bank Name

Enter the name of the bank in which the transactions are made.

Branch Name

Enter the name of the branch of the bank in which the transactions are made.

Amount

Enter the property tax amount due. 

Paid By

Enter the name of the person who makes the payment.

NOTE: Click Add More to enter details of another cheque or DD. This is applicable when the citizen pays the property tax via multiple cheques or DDs.

 

5.    Click one of the following options:

·    Create: To generate a receipt

NOTE: You can choose to print the receipt. A copy of the receipt has to be given to the citizen.
 

·    Reset: To clear the information and re-enter

 

 WARNING: If there is a cheque bounce, then the property owner cannot make the next payment by cheque or cash. This payment can be done only by DD.

The minimum amount to be paid through DD can be greater or equal to the sum of the cheque bounce amount and the penalty charges as shown in the equation below:

DD Payment Amount >= Cheque Bounce Amount + Penalty Amount
