Modifying Street Rate

Previous Top 

 

A ward comprises of multiple streets. The Corporation has fixed a specific tax rate for a specific street depending on the developmental activities in and around the street. A street can belong to a specific category depending on its tax rate. For example, all streets that charge 0.5 rupees per square feet for residential properties belong to the category K12.

 

The Corporation has the power to alter the tax rate of a specific street at its discretion.

 

To modify the tax rate associated with a specific street, perform the following steps:

1.    Click Property Tax from the left navigation bar.

2.    Click PTIS_Administration.

3.    Click Change Street Rate to display the Change Street Rate page.

4.    From the Zone drop-down list, select the zone to which the street belongs.

5.    From the Ward drop-down list, select the ward to which the street belongs.

6.    From the Street Name drop-down list, select the street whose tax rate you want to modify.

7.    Click Search. 

 

You get a page which lists the tax rate which is currently applied to

properties (both residential and nonresidential) that belong to the street.

        

8.    In the Revised Rate field, enter the revised tax rate (Rupees per square feet) against the usage of the property. When you change the tax rate of a street, the category to which the street belongs changes.

9.    From the Revised Category drop-down list, select the new category to which the street belongs.

 

NOTE: The Revised Category drop-down list automatically gets populated based on the revised tax rate you have entered.

 

10.        Click Change Street Rate.

11.        You get an acknowledgement page with the revised tax rates.

12.        Click Confirm to save the changes  (or click Back to go to the previous page and make changes.).


An acknowledgement page is displayed confirming the change.
