Logging On to the PTIS by the Officials

Previous Top Next 

The revenue officials who can log on to the PTIS are as follows:

·      Assistant

·      Assistant HQ (Headquarter)

·      Tax Collector

·      Assessor

·      Assessor HQ 

·      Assistant Revenue Officer (ARO)

·      Assistant Revenue Officer HQ (ARO HQ)

·      Zonal Officer (ZO)

·      Additional Revenue Officer HQ

·      Revenue Officer (RO)

·      Deputy Commissioner Revenues (DCR)

 

To log on to the PTIS application, perform the following steps:

1.   Open a Web browser.

 

NOTE: The PTIS application is compatible with either Internet Explorer or Mozilla Firefox Web Browsers.

 

2.   Enter the address of the PTIS application home page in the Address bar, and then press ENTER  to display the login page.

3.   Enter your user name in the UserName field.

4.   Enter your password in the Password field.

5.   Click Login to display the PTIS homepage.

 

NOTE: If you are unable to login, contact your administrator for further help. 


NOTE: The menu options that are displayed depend on the role that has been assigned to the respective user based on authorization and responsibilities.
