Approving a Property

Previous Top Next 

The integrity of a property needs to be verified and approved by the higher officials before performing any action on it.


They must verify the data when:

·      a new property is registered

·      a property undergoes division

·      a property undergoes amalgamation

·      the ownership of a property undergoes any change


The officials can choose to either approve or reject the property at their discretion based on the information furnished. When they reject a property, the property goes back to the Inbox of the officer who sends it for approval.


The number of approval cycles depends on the built-up area and usage of the property. See work flow for more information on approval cycles.


To approve a property, perform the following steps:

1.   Log on to the PTIS application. You get the Inbox where all tasks that need your approval are listed. The Inbox includes the following information:

·    Date and time at which you received the task in your Inbox

·    Position and user name of the sender

·    Name of the task

·    Status

·    Bill number of the property

2.   Click the name of the task you want to approve. You get the approval page displaying all property details which you can verify for its correctness.

3.   Click one of the following options:

·    Approve: To approve the property. You get a confirmation page where you need to click Approve once again.

·    Reject: To reject the property. You get a confirmation page where you need to click Reject once again.

NOTE: Before rejecting a property, you must enter a comment indicating the reason for rejection in the Comment field.

4.   Click one of the following options:

·    Print: To print the acknowledgement page.

·    Close: To close the acknowledgement page.
