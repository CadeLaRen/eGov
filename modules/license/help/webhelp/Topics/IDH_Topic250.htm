Manual Override: Select this check box if you want to enter the AV amount and tax amount. (based on which the revised tax is calculated. Revised tax calculated based on the AV amount will override the tax calculated based on all other parameters. The revised half-yearly tax will be 12.4% of the AV amount)

NOTE: You must type the name of the owner exactly as it is given in the form submitted. Once you register the property with a wrong name, you cannot change it, and you need to transfer the ownership of such property to the new name.

 

 

Demand Effective Installment field gets populated with the half-yearly installment from which onwards the tax is due.

 

North By: The property number to the north direction of the property being registered.
