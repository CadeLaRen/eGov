Approve Hawker License

Previous Top Next 

To approve a Hawker license, perform the following steps:

1.      Log on to the application.

2.      Inbox lists the tasks to be perform.

3.      Click the relevant task to open and approve license page.

4.      Verify the adequacy of the details and decide whether to approve it or not.

5.      If you decide reject the Hawker license, Enter the reasons in the Comments field.

 

The officials can choose to either approve or reject the property at their discretion based on the information furnished. When they reject a property, the property goes back to the Inbox of the officer who sends it for approval.

 

·         Click one of the following steps:

·         Approve: To approve the hawker license.

·         Reject: To reject the hawker license.

·         Print Preview: To take a print copy of the page.

·         Close: To close the page.
