An asset is an item of economic value owned by the urban local bodies (ULBs) that could be converted to cash. For example: Land, buildings, vehicles, roads, bridges and the like.

 

The ULBs need to record all the transactions that occur, in order to track the quantity and value of the assets held, at any point in time. When transactions are not recorded in an organized fashion, it could result in a general lack of control and loss of money.

 

eGov Asset Management System is developed exclusively for managing the assets in the ULBs. The system supports all the major operations on an asset over its lifecycle:

·        Capitalization//////

·        Revaluation/////////

·        Depreciation//////////

·        Sale or Disposal of the Asset///

 

///////////////////

Asset Capitalization: Capitalization of an Asset on the completion of a Work Order or on the Receipt of an Asset (in case of Purchase of Assets).

Asset Revaluation: Revaluation whenever any asset improvement Work Order is completed or an asset revaluation/

Asset Disposal/Sale: Financial Transactions related to the disposal and/or Sale of an asset, including recording the book profits or losses, as the case maybe.

Asset Depreciation: Based on pre-defined rules, depreciation of an Asset with the relevant accounting entries.
