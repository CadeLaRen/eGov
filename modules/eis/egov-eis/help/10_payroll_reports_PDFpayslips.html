PDF Payslips

The following details are displayed in the PDF payslips:

  • Employee Name
  • Month/Year of Payslip
  • Designation
  • Bank Account Number
  • Center
  • Earnings and Allowances--breakup of individual earnings and allowances and their total
  • Deductions--breakup of individual deductions and their total
  • Loan Recoveries--breakup of individual loan and amount recovered on each
  • Net Pay
