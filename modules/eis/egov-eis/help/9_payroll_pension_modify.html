Modify Pension Details

1. You can modify the following pension details of the employee:

  • Disbursement type
  • Bank details, if the disbursement type is Bank Transfer
  • First name of the nominee
  • Middle name of the nominee
  • Last name of the nominee
  • Relationship of the nominee with the employee
  • Whether the nominee is alive
  • Whether the nominee is married
  • Date of birth of the nominee
  • Whether the nominee is employed
  • Method of disbursal of the pension amount

See Create Pension for field descriptions.

2. You can add additional employee nominees. To do this, click Add Row.

3. Click Save to save the details you modified.
