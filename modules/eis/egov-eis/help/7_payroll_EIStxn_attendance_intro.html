Employee Attendance

Leave types and the number of days for each leave type are created in the Leave Master.

Leave types are based on the designation of an employee.

Employee attendance is awarded and calculated based on the calendar year. At the beginning of each calendar year, the leave that an employee is eligible for is credited to his or her leave balance. At the end of the calendar year, the leave closing balance is calculated and those leave types that can be accumulated are carried forward to the next financial year.

View Attendance Register

Employee attendance is tracked in the Attendance Register. The Attendance Register is created for each month of a given year. Attendance details impact an employee's salary, and employee attendance is sent to payroll.

To view the Attendance Register:

1. In the eGov EIS Payroll home page, click EIS-Payroll.

2. Click EIS.

3. Under EIS, click Attendance and then click Create.

4. In the Search Employee screen, specify the month for which you want to view the attendance.

5. Select one of the following:

  • To view the attendance of an entire department,click to select it from the drop-down list beside Department.
  • To view the attendance details of all employees, ensure that no details are filled in any of the fields.

6. Click Submit. The Attendance Register that corresponds to the options you entered is displayed.

NOTE: If the search results yield over 100 records, an alert is displayed. The search needs to be refined so that fewer than 100 records are obtained.
