Compensatory Off Leave

Compensatory Off (or CompOff) leave is awarded to employees who have worked on holidays.

All the holidays of the organisation are indicated in the Attendance Register. They are identified by the letter H in the column below the date of the holiday. An employee who worked on a holiday is eligible to avail leave on a regular working day in lieu of the holiday worked.

Employee attendance on a holiday needs to be recorded in the Attendance Register by marking the employee present on a specific holiday.
