Create Compensatory Off

To create a Compensatory Off leave, it is necessary that the date of the holiday that the employee worked is recorded in the Attendance Register. The employee should also be eligible for this type of leave. The leave types for which an employee is eligible are created in the Leave Master.

Record date of Holiday Worked

1. In the eGov EIS Payroll home page, click EIS-Payroll.

2. Click EIS.

3. Under EIS, click Attendance and then click Create.

4. Make sure that you specify the month for which you want to mark the compensatory off.

5. Search for the employee for whom a compensatory off needs to be marked.

6. On the date of the holiday when the employee worked, mark the letter P.

7. Click Save. You now see a pop-up with the message: Provide a CompOff?

8. You can now:

  • Click OK to indicate that a compensatory off needs to be provided.

    Or

  • Click Cancel if no compensatory off should be given.

NOTE: If you click OK the employee's leave credit for the leave type Compensatory Off is incremented by 1. This is important as such credit to the available leave of type CompOff is required when it becomes necessary to approve the CompOff holiday that the employee applies for.

Approve CompOff Leave

An employee needs to avail of a CompOff holiday within 3 months of the date of the holiday that was worked. A CompOff holiday can only be given to an employee if they are eligible for this type of leave (configured in the Leave Master) and they have available leave credit.

To approve a CompOff, you must have the necessary hierarchy privileges.

1. In the EIS menu, select Leave Application to view different leave application options.

2. Click Approve Compensatory Off. This opens the Search CompOff to Approve screen.

3. Enter the Employee code if known, or other search criteria and click Submit.

4. From the Search Results, click the employee for whom the Compensatory Off holiday is to be approved. This shows the following:

  • CompOff Date
  • Employee Name
  • Employee Code
  • Status
  • Masters Column

5. In the Masters column, click approve. This opens the CompOff Approval screen where you can select the date on which the employee wants to take a compensatory off day.

6. In the CompOff Approval screen, the name of the employee, the employee code, and the date of the holiday worked are displayed. You can enter the following:

  • CompOff Date: The regular working day when the employee wants to avail a holiday.
  • Select the status of this leave request from the drop-down list. To approve the CompOff holiday, select Approved.

7. Click Save to store details of the CompOff and status.

8. To discard the details entered, click Close.
