Create a Disciplinary Action

To do this, enter the following details in the Disciplinary Punishments screen:

1. In the Nature of Allegations field, describe the offence committed by the employee.

2. In the Charge Memo Number field, type the Memo Number from the register that has filed the offence.

3. In the Charge Memo Date field, type the date the allegation was filed.

4. In the Charge Memo Served field, type the date the Memo was assigned to the Enquiry Officer.

5. If the employee is charged with attendance related issues, and the employee claims to be indisposed due to sickness, accident, operation, and so on type the nature of the problem in the Nature of Disposal (if Disposed) field.

6. In the Unauthorised Absent field:

  • Click Yes if the employee has taken unauthorised leave. Type the corresponding From date and To date in the dd/mm/yyyy format.
  • Click No if the employee has taken unauthorised leave.

7. In the Whether Suspended field:

  • Click Yes if the employee is suspended as part of the disciplinary action. Type the corresponding From date and To date in the dd/mm/yyyy format.
  • Click No if the employee is not suspended as part of the disciplinary action.

8. In the Enquiry Officer Id field, type the Employee ID of the Enquiry Officer who is assigned an enquiry into the allegation and decide an appropriate disciplinary action. The Enquiry Officer Name and the EO's Designation are displayed automatically.

9. In the EO Nominated Date field, type the date the Enquiry Officer is nominated to make the enquiry.

10. In the EO Report Date field, type the date the Enquiry Officer files a report regarding the enquiry and the associated disciplinary action.

NOTE: To add another Enquiry Officer, click Add Row.

11. In the Punishment Order Date, type the date when the decision to give a disciplinary action is taken.

12. In the Unauthorised absent period regulation field, type the time period for when the employee had taken leave. This is applicable for attendance related issues.

13. In the Nature of Punishment field, type the punishment given to the employee for committing the offence.

14. In the Punishment Effective Date field, type the date from when the punishment is effective.

15. Click Save to save the details.
