Pay Summary Report

The Pay Summary report shows employee pay details for a given financial year. When this report is displayed for the current financial year, the details shown are for all the months of the finanical year till date.

The following details are displayed in the Pay Summary

  • Employee Code
  • Employee Name
  • Employee Designation
  • Date of Joining

For each month in the year that is applicable:

  • Earnings and Allowances--breakup of individual earnings and allowances and their total
  • Deductions--breakup of individual deductions including loan recoveries and their total
  • Net Pay
