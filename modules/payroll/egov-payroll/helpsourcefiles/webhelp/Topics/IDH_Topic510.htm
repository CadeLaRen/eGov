Approving a Task (TBD)

 

To approve a task, perform the following steps:

 

1.   Log on to the Payroll application. You get the Inbox where all tasks that need your approval are listed. The Inbox includes the following information:

 

·  Date and time at which you received the task in your Inbox

·  Position and user name of the sender

·  Name of the task

·  Status of the task
 

2.   Click the name of the task you want to approve. You get the approval page displaying all task details which you can verify for its correctness.

3.   Click one of the following options:
 

·  Save: The task gets saved in your Drafts folder. You can make the necessary changes and then submit it. The task gets forwarded to the respective higher official.
