About Employee Payslip Approval

After employee payslips are generated (manually or by batch generation), the payslips have to be approved. Only a ULB employee with the required privileges can approve a payslip. This would typically be the employee's superior. For this reason, the Approve Payslips appears as an Inbox item for the employee's superior. Typically, eGov EIS Payroll has three levels of approval for an employee payslip.
