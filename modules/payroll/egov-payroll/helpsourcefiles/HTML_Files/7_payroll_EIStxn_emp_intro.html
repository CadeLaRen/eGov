EIS Transactions

The Employee Information System (EIS) maintains employment details and attendance of all employees in the organisation in the form of employee records.

Employee Record

Employee records need to be created and updated periodically to serve as a repository of current information about all the employees.

Employee records consist of the following information:

  • Employee Details: Include personal information of employees such as full name, family details, date of birth, religion, address, and so on.
  • Assignment Details: Include details of designation, position, department, grade, and so on.
  • Service History: Includes details about probation, regularization, and so on.
  • Other Details: Include details of educational qualification, details of properties employees currently possess, and so on.

You can create, view, and modify employee records in eGov EIS.

To create, view, or modify employee records in EIS:

1. In the EIS menu, select Employee.

2. Click one of the following to perform the related action:

  • Create to open the Employee Details screen.
  • View to open the Search Employee to View screen.
  • Modify to open the Search Employee to Modify screen.
