View Advance Disbursement (Cheque) Details

1. In the View Advance screen, view the following details:

  • Voucher No.
  • Name of the cashier
  • Banks
  • Account number
  • Cheque Number
  • Cheque/DD Date

See Create Cheque Disbursement Details for field descriptions.

2. After you have viewed the details, click Close to exit the screen.
