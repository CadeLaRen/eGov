Verify Gratuity and Pension Details

The employee's Inbox contains the following for individual departments:

  • Details of gratuity and pension calculations for former employees to whom this is applicable, before they are sent for approval.
  • Details of gratuity and pension calculations for former employees to whom this is applicable, when the latter are rejected by the employee's superior.

Details for each department are displayed with the corresponding number of tasks. A task indicates the number of gratuity and pension records that are pending approval in each department.

To verify gratuity and pension details:

1. Click the link under the Task folder for the relevant department name. This displays details such as:

  • Employee Name
  • Department
  • Pension Number
  • Bill Number
  • Gratuity Amount
  • Net Payment

NOTE: Click on the View link to check the history of the employee gratuity and pension record.

2. Click on the link in the relevant row of the Employee Name column to open the View Gratuity screen. Verify the total disbursement amount. The following details can be edited:

  • Disbursement Type
  • Bank
  • Bank Branch
  • Bank Account
  • Pension Commuted (%)
  • Monthly Pension Payable

See Create Gratuity for field descriptions.

3. Do one of the following:

  • If any details were modified, click Save.
  • If no details were modified, click Close.

4. You can do one of the following:

  • Click on the relevant row to select the Accept check box for approval. The details are sent to the employee's superior for approval.
  • Click on the relevant row to select the Reject check box to reject the approval request. This deletes all the gratuity and pension details for the employee.

5. In the Observation field, you can type comments such as the reason for rejection or approval.

6. Click Submit. The following message is displayed: Executed Successfully.
