Managing Bulk Updation of Payheads

When you need to update payhead details of all the employees in your organization, instead of changing each employee's payhead details individually, you can update them globally.

For example, the government has announced a Daily Allowance (DA) hike of 1000 rupees for all employees of the ULBs for a specific month on account of a festival. In such situations, you can update the DA hike in bulk.
