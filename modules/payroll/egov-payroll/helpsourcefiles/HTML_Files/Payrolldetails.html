Assigning/Modifying Employee Payscales

You need to record the payroll details such as basic amount, increment amount, date of joining of the employee, annual increment date, and so on of employees who have been created in the system.

To enter the payroll details or modify the details that you have entered, perform the following steps:

1. In the Payroll menu, click Employee-Payroll

2. Click Modify to create/modify the payscale details of the employee.

Employee Search screen is displayed.

3. Enter the details to search for the employee whose payscale details you want to create/modify as described in Table 1.

4. Click Submit.

Depending on the search criteria, a list of employee records is displayed. You can see the name, employee code, designation and department of each of the employees.

5. To create/modify the payscale of an employee, click the Modify link in the relevant row.

Payroll Details Page is displayed.

6. Enter the details as described in Table 2.

7. Click one of the following options:

  • Save: To save the payscale assigned/modified
  • Close: To close the page without saving the details

Table 1: Finding an Employee Record
Field Description
Designation Select the designation of the employee from the Designation drop-down list.
Department Select the department of the employee from the Department drop-down list.
Employee Code Type the employee code.
Employee Name Type the name of the employee.
Status Select the status of the employee from the Status drop-down list
Functionary Select the functionary from the Functionary drop-down list.
Type Enter the type of employment from the Type drop-down list.

Payscale Details

*Monthlypay Type the monthly salary of the employee.

Stagnant Pay Stagnation increment is the concept of giving increment to an employee on reaching the max limit of the payscale – Instead of assigning a new payscale.

Select the Stagnant Pay check box if you want to increase the basic pay of an employee whose payscale has reached the maximum limit. Such employees will get a fixed increment every month till a new payscale is assigned to them through a pay commission.You can change the basic amount for such employees above their payscale range.

NOTE: To add/delete a Payscale row, click Add Row/Delete Row.
