About Gratuity Disbursal

After gratuity details are approved, the gratuity can be disbursed to the employee. To create a record of gratuity disbursement details:

1. In the EIS-Payroll menu, click Payroll.

2. Click Pension and then click Disburse Gratuity.

3. Do one of the following:

  • Click Create to create gratuity disbursal details.
  • Click View to view gratuity details.
