Leave Application

It is a regular practice in all organizations that employees should apply for leave in advance when possible. However, only employees with the required authority can create their own leave applications. Other employees need to inform their superiors of proposed leave so that the corresponding leave application can be created.

In eGov EIS Payroll, it is possible to create, view, and cancel an employee leave application. Leave applications are automatically approved when they are created. For this reason, no separate approval of a leave application is required.

Leave applications can be cancelled and a fresh application made in situations such as when an employee applies for 5 days leave but actually takes only 3 days leave.

Information of approved leave days is used during payslip generation.

There are two steps to create, view, or cancel a leave application:

1. Search for the employee for whom the leave application needs to be made, viewed, or canceled.

2. Do one of the following:

  • Create a leave application by filling details in the Leave Application form
  • View a leave application
  • Cancel a leave application

To search for the employee:

1. In the EIS menu, select Leave Application.

2. Click one of the following:

  • Apply and Approve open the Search Leave Application to Create screen.
  • View to open the Search Leave Application to View screen.
  • Cancel to open the Search Leave Application to Cancel screen

3. The search criteria can be based on:

  • Designation: Click the designation of the employee from the drop-down list.
  • Department: Click the department of the employee from the drop-down list.
  • Employee Code: Type the employee code.
  • Employee Name: Type the employee name.
  • Search All (Active and Inactive): Click Yes to search for all employees. Click No to search for only active employees.

4. Click Submit to search for the employee account. The records of employees who have applied for leave and satisfy the search criteria are displayed.

5. Do one of the following:

  • Click Create to apply for leave
  • Click View to view the leave application
  • Click Modify to cancel the leave application
