Managing User-Defined Payhead Rules

Occasionally, the standard calculation types such as Slab Based, Monthly Flat Rate, and Computed Value may not suit your requirement of defining a payhead.

For example, assume that you may want to define a payhead "DA" that needs to be calculated as (10% of Basic + 5% of HRA), and another payhead "PF" as (12% of Basic + 750).

In such cases, you can define a customized rule to compute payheads to suit your business requirements.
