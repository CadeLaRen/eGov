Advance Disbursement

Sanctioned advances can be disbursed to the employee by cheque, cash, or in the form of a direct bank transfer. Disbursement is for the entire advance amount, and no partial payments will be made.

When an advance is disbursed, the monthly payment towards the advance amount is recovered as a deduction in subsequent employee payslips.

Disbursement by Cheque

When an advance is disbursed by cheque, a cheque needs to be generated for every employee. You can create, view, or modify cheque disbursement details.

To manage cheque disbursement details:

1. In the Payroll menu, select Advance Disbursement.

2. Click By Cheque, then click one of the following:

  • Create to create details for disbursement by cheque
  • View to view cheque disbursement details
  • Modify to modify cheque disbursement details

3. In the Employee Code drop-down list, click on the Employee Code. All the employees that have sanctioned advances with the disbursement type as cheque are displayed.

4. Click Submit.

See Create Cheque Disbursement details, View Cheque Disbursement details, or Modify Cheque Disbursement details.

Disbursement by Cash or Direct Bank Transfer

When advance disbursement is done by cash or by direct bank transfer, a single disbursement voucher is created for all the employees with a common Advance Type and Fund. For example, if a sanctioned advance exists for ten employees that are assigned Adhoc Advances (Advance Type) from the General Fund (name of the Fund), only one voucher is created in eGov EIS Payroll. This voucher contains details of the total advance amount that needs to be given to these ten employees.

After the voucher is passed, the total amount is finally disbursed among the employees by cash or by bank transfer.

To create disbursement details for cash or for direct bank transfers, do the following:

1. Search for the advance disbursement based on the:

  • Advance Type
  • Name of the Fund
  • Disbursement method
  • Department

2. Click Search to display the Create Disbursement by Cash/Direct Transfer screen. All the employees that have sanctioned advances with the disbursement type as cash or direct transfer are displayed. See Create Cash Disbursement.

To view or modify cash disbursal and direct bank transfer details:

1. Search for the advance disbursement based on the:

  • Advance Type
  • Name of the Fund
  • Disbursement method

2. Click Search to View Cash Disbursement details and Modify Cash Disbursement details.
