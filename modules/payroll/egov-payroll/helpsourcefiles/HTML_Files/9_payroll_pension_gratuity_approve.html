Approve Gratuity and Pension Disbursal Details

The Inbox of the superior of the employee who created pension and gratuity details contains the following for individual departments:

  • Details of gratuity and pension calculations for former employees to whom this is applicable, that are sent for approval.

All gratuity and pension records that are pending approval are identified by the department name that is prefixed with the following message: Approve Gratuity

Details for each department are displayed with the corresponding number of tasks. A task indicates the number of gratuity and pension records that are pending approval in each department.

To approve gratuity and pension details:

1. Under the Task folder, click the relevant department name. This link opens a page with details such as:

  • Employee's Name
  • Department
  • Pension Number
  • Bill Number
  • Gratuity Amount
  • Net Payment

2. To view the history of the employee gratuity and pension record, click View.

3. To view gratuity and pension calculations for a particular employee, click his or her name in the Employee Name column. This displays the View Gratuity screen. See Create Gratuity for field descriptions.

4. Click Close to close the View Gratuity screen.

5. You can do one of the following:

  • Click on the relevant row to select the Accept check box for approval. The gratuity and pension details are sent for disbursal.
  • Click on the relevant row to select the Reject check box to reject the approval request. The gratuity and pension details are reverted back to the employee who created the gratuity and pension details.

6. In the Observation field, you can type comments related to the reason for rejection or approval.

7. Click Submit. The following message is displayed: Executed Successfully.

A payment voucher is generated if the superior approves the gratuity and pension details.
