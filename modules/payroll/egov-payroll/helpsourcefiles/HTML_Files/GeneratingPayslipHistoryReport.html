Generating a Payslip History Report

A Payslip History Report is a report on employees/Pensioners payhistory across years. ie, you can generate a report on the payslip details of an employee between two given periods. For instance, an employee joined on the 1st of June 2000. You can use an employee payslip history report to see the payslip details of the employee from the day he joined till date.

To generate a payslip history report, perform the following steps:

1. In the Payroll menu, click Reports.

2. Click Payslip Exception Report.

Payslip History Report page is displayed.

3. Type the employee code of the employee-whose payslip history you want to view-in the Employee Code field.

4. Select the month and year-from which you want to view the report-in the Month and Year drop-down lists respectively.

5. Select the month and year-till which you want to view the report-in the Month and Year drop-down lists respectively.

6. Click Submit.

The payslip history of the employee is displayed. You can see the following details:

  • Employee Code
  • Employee Name
  • Date of Joining
  • Earnings and Allowances in each of the months--breakup of individual earnings and allowances and their total
  • Deductions in each of the months--breakup of individual deductions including loan recoveries and their total
  • Net Pay

7. To export the payslip history report to excel/pdf, click the Excel/PDF link
