Modify Disciplinary Action

To modify the disciplinary action assigned to an employee:

1. Click on the link in the Masters column for the row that belongs to the employee.

2. The following details can be modified:

  • Nature of Allegations
  • Charge Memo Number
  • Charge Memo Date
  • Charge Memo Served Date
  • Nature of Disposal
  • Unauthorised Absent
  • Whether Suspended
  • Whether Subsistence Paid
  • Enquiry Officer Details
  • Punishment Order Date
  • Nature of Punishment
  • Punishment Effective Date

Descriptions of these fields are available in Create a Disciplinary Action.

3. After you modify the details, click Close.
