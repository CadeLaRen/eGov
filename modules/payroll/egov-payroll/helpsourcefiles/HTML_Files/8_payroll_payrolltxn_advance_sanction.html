Sanction an Advance

In the Sanction Advance screen:

1.Verify the advance details. The following fields can be modified:

  • Sanction Amount
  • Number of Installments
  • Interest Amount
  • Monthly Payment
  • Payment Method

See Create Advance for field descriptions.

2. In the Sanction No. field type the sanction number for this particular advance.

3. Click the calendar icon beside the Sanction/Rejection Date field, to specify the date of the sanction.

4. The Sanction/Reject By field contains the login ID of the person who has logged into eGov Payroll.

5. You can now do one of the following:

  • Click sanction to sanction this advance. The message Advance sanctioned successfully is displayed.
  • Click sanction and next to sanction this advance and go on to sanction another advance.
  • Click reject to reject this advance application.
