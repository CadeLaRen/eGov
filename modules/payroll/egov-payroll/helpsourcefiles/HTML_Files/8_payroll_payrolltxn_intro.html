Handling Payroll Transactions

eGov EIS Payroll is a fund-based accounting system that generates the salary payslips for the employees in an organization. It calculates the payslip of each employee based on the Payscale and Payhead assigned to each of them. Employee payslips are processed manually or generated in batches and sent for approval. For more information, see Managing Employee Payslip.

eGov Payroll system helps to manage the salary advances awarded to an employee and also to maintain employee Provident Fund details.
