Create Cash Disbursement Details

In the Create Disbursement By Cash/Direct Transfer screen, do the following:

1. In the Voucher No. field, type the voucher number.

2. Type the Voucher Date in the dd/mm/yyyy format. You can also click on the calendar icon to select the date.

3. In the Paid By drop-down list, click the relevant cashier name.

4. From the Bank drop-down list, select the bank from which the advance amount needs to be debited.

5. From the Bank Account drop-down list, select the associated bank account. The Balance amount is displayed.

6. Click the arrow mark to display the next available cheque number from the cheque book that is used currently.

7. Type the Cheque/DD date in the dd/mm/yyyy format. You can also click on the calendar icon to select the date.

8. Click one of the following:

  • Save & Close: To save the advance disbursement details and close the screen.
  • Save & New: To save the advance disbursement details and create a new disbursement record.
  • Save & View: To save the advance disbursement details and view the disbursement details.
  • Cancel: To clear all the entries in the screen.
  • Show GL Entry: To display the General Ledger entries for the advance.
  • Close: To close the screen without saving details.

NOTE: To view the list of employees for whom the advance disbursement voucher needs to be created, click on the Sanctioned Amount link.
