Viewing or Modifying Nomination Head Master

 

To view or modify a Nomination Head Master, perform the following steps:

 

1.   In the Payroll menu, click Nomination Head Master.

2.   Click View/Edit. The Nomination Header List page is displayed.

3.   From the PayHead drop-down list, select a payhead that you want to view or modify.

4.   You can do one of the following:

·      To view a Nomination Head Master, click View. The View Nomination Header page is displayed.

·      To modify the details of a Nomination head Master, click Edit and make the changes in the associated fields.

5.   Click Save if you have modified the Nomination Head Master details.

6.   Click Close to close the page.
