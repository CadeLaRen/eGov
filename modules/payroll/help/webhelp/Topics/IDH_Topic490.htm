Introduction

 

When a task is created by the concerned official, it goes to the Inbox of the sanctioning officers for approval process. The officials can choose to either approve or reject a task at their discretion based on the information furnished. When they reject a task, the respective task goes back to the Inbox of the officer who sends it for approval. The approving officer needs to leave a comment before rejecting a task.
 

There can be as many number of approval cycles as you want depending on your business requirements, the type of task and the employee information. After each approval cycle, the status of the task changes.
