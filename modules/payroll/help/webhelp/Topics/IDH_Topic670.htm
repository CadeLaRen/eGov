Introduction

 

There are various allowances that are allotted to the employees. Some of these allowances are based on the the payslip entity. For example, payhead  like PF is based on the payslip item. Some allowances such as DCRG (Death Cum Retirement Gratuity) and Group insurance are not necessarily be the payslip item, hence the master list of payheads for which nominations exists has to be created and maintained.
