To disburse pension and gratuity when an employee retires or dies before retirement, the following need to be done:

 

1. Update employee status in the Modify Employee Details screen.

 

2. Create a Pension record.

 

3. Compute the total amount that needs to be disbursed. This is based on all of the following:

 

    * Pension

    * Gratuity or commuted gratuity as applicable

    * Unpaid Loan amounts

 

4. Obtain approval from a superior in the ULB.

 

5. Record details for disbursal of pension.

 

6. Disburse pension to employee or eligible nominees.

 

Employee Dies After Retirement

 

When an employee dies after retirement and eligible nominees are present, such nominees continue to receive the total pension amount in proportionate shares. It must be remembered that the gratuity was paid at the time of retirement. For this reason, steps to be followed are:

 

1. Update employee status in the Modify Employee Details screen.

 

2. Update Pension record to reflect nominees

 

3. Compute pension amounts to be disbursed to each of the eligible nominees. (The total pension amount originally calculated is divided among the eligible nominees.)

 

4. Obtain approval from a superior in the ULB.

 

5. Disburse pension to eligible nominees.

Update Employee Status

 

Since employees can receive pension only if they are retired or deceased, the employee status needs to be updated in the eGovernment application. To update the employee status:

 

1. In the EIS-Payroll menu, click EIS.

 

2. Click Employee.

 

3. Click Modify to open the Search Employee screen.

 

4. Search for the employee based on any of the following criteria:

 

    * Designation

    * Department

    * Employee Code

    * Employee Name

    * Employee Status

 

5. In the Modify Employee screen, do the following:

 

a. In the Employee Status drop-down list, click one of the following:

 

    * Retired: Select this when the employee is retired.

    * Deceased: Select this when the employee is deceased.

 

b. In the Modification Summary field, type a brief description of the modifications you have made.

 

6. Click Save to save the details.

Create Pension Details

 

After the employee status is updated, you need to check if the pension details exist for the employee. Pension details include nominee details and disbursement method for the employee.

 

If pension details do not exist or have to be updated, do the following:

 

1. In the EIS-Payroll menu, click EIS.

 

2. Click Pension.

 

3. Click Create, View, or Modify to open the Search Employee screen.

 

4. Search for the employee based on any of the following criteria:

 

    * Designation

    * Department

    * Employee Code

    * Employee Name

    * Employee Status

 

5. Click Submit. This lists the employees that satisfy the search criteria.

 

6. Do one of the following:

 

    * Click Create to define pension details for the employee.Select this when no previous record exists.

    * Click View to view pension details of the employee. Select this to view pension details for an employee.

    * Click Modify to modify pension details of the employee. Select this when the pension details need to be updated.

 

 

COC has various heads for which nominations are maintained. Allowances like GPF, SPFGS, CPS, DCRG, GI – Group Insurance, Commutation, Pension are some of the heads which requires nomination particulars. These allowances will be paid to the nominees on death of the employee.  An Employee can nominate more than one nominee for a head. Employee can also mention the percentage of share against each nominee or can choose to leave without entering percentage, also can enter percentage for one nominee and leave remaining percentage to be shared among the remaining nominees. Examples are given in the percentage details section below.

 

 

Nominee Masters –  (Employee – Nominee mapping)

Each employee can have all the nominees as his master list and choose from nominees list for each of the head while nominating and updating percentage details. This will be the master list of nominees for all employees.
