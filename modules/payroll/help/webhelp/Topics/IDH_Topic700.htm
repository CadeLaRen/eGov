Modifying Employee Pension Header Data

 

To modify the employee pension data, perform the following steps:

1.   From the Payroll menu, click Pension.

2.   From the Employee Master list, click View/Modify. The Payroll Search Screen page is displayed.

3.   Specify the details required to find an employee as described in Table1.

4.   Click Submit. A list of employees and their details are displayed in a grid according to the search criteria.

5.   Click Modify from the Masters column. The Employee Pension Details page is displayed.TBD
